Academics

Over the years, Prince Edward School has gained a reputation for academic, cultural and sporting excellence, producing men of integrity and value who have become widely influential in Zimbabwe, Africa, and the rest of the world. Today the school strives to maintain and even surpass this high standard.

Academic Life

Prince Edward School strives to educate and nurture our boys into the best they can be and bring out their full potential. To this end we always strive to broaden their outlook with a rich and diverse academic curriculum catered for life at the school and after.
